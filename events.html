    Losar Festival

    Tibetan New Year Celebration

    February 28 to 2 March, 2025 3 Days Rumtek Monastery

    Losar is the most important festival in Tibetan Buddhism, marking the beginning of the new year. The festival features traditional dances, prayers, and colorful ceremonies. Visitors can witness the spectacular Cham dances performed by monks in elaborate costumes and masks.

    Festival

    Saga Dawa

    Buddha's Birth, Enlightenment & Parinirvana

    May 28, 2025 Full Day All Monasteries

    Saga Dawa is one of the most sacred days in Buddhism, commemorating three major events in Buddha's life: his birth, enlightenment, and parinirvana. Special prayers, butter lamp offerings, and circumambulation ceremonies are held throughout the day.

    Ceremony

    Tsechu Festival

    Masked Dance Performance

    October 2-4, 2025 3 Days Enchey Monastery

    The Tsechu Festival features spectacular masked dances performed by monks, depicting various Buddhist deities and stories. These sacred dances are believed to bring blessings and ward off evil spirits. The festival includes colorful costumes, traditional music, and elaborate rituals.

    Dance

    Guru Rinpoche Day

    Celebration of Padmasambhava

    July 22, 2024 Full Day Pemayangtse Monastery

    Guru Rinpoche Day celebrates the birth of Padmasambhava, the great master who brought Buddhism to Tibet. Special prayers, offerings, and teachings are conducted. This is considered one of the most auspicious days for spiritual practice and receiving blessings.

    Ceremony

    Lhabab Duchen

    Buddha's Descent from Heaven

    November 6, 2024 Full Day All Monasteries

    Lhabab Duchen commemorates Buddha's descent from the Tushita heaven after teaching his mother. This is one of the four great festivals in Tibetan Buddhism. Special prayers and offerings are made, and the merit of all actions is multiplied many times on this day.
