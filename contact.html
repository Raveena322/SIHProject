Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a permit to visit monasteries?

Most monasteries in Sikkim are open to visitors without special permits. However, some remote monasteries may require prior permission. We can help arrange necessary permits if needed.

What is the best time to visit?

The best time to visit Sikkim monasteries is from March to May and September to November when the weather is pleasant and mountain views are clear.

Are there guided tours available?

Yes, we offer guided tours with experienced local guides who can provide insights into the history, culture, and spiritual significance of each monastery.

What should I wear when visiting?

Dress modestly and respectfully. Avoid shorts, sleeveless tops, and revealing clothing. It's also customary to remove shoes before entering prayer halls.

Can I take photographs?

Photography is generally allowed in monastery courtyards and exteriors, but may be restricted inside prayer halls. Always ask for permission and respect the monks' privacy.

How do I get to Sikkim?

Sikkim is accessible by road from West Bengal. The nearest airport is in Bagdogra, and the nearest railway station is in New Jalpaiguri. We can arrange transportation from these points.
